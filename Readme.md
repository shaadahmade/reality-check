# 🔍 RealityCheck — Your Idea vs The Real World

> *Stop falling in love with your idea. Start finding out if the world will.*

**RealityCheck** is a Claude AI Skill that combines deep research, a structured interview, and brutal critique into one automated workflow — so you know if your idea is actually worth building before you waste months on it.

---

## ✨ What It Does

Most people validate ideas by talking to friends who say "that's cool!" RealityCheck does the opposite.

It acts as a **VC, product strategist, research analyst, and devil's advocate** — all in one session.

```
Your Idea → Interview → Research → Critique → Verdict
```

---

## 🔄 The 3-Phase Flow

### Phase 1 — The Interview 🎤
Claude interviews you in smart batches of questions across 6 areas:
- The idea itself (what exactly is it?)
- The market (who, how big, who else?)
- Why it works (core insight, user validation)
- The builder (why you? what's your edge?)
- Feasibility (MVP, hard parts, funding)
- Confidence calibration (are you realistic or delusional?)

> **Secret mechanic**: It tracks how overconfident you sound. The more certain you seem, the harsher Phase 3 gets. 😬

---

### Phase 2 — Deep Research 🔬
While you wait, Claude autonomously runs **10–15 searches** across:

| Source | What It Looks For |
|--------|-------------------|
| 🌐 General Web | Landscape, existing solutions, recent news |
| 📰 News | Market trends, funding activity, failures |
| 🎓 Academic Papers | Market data, technical feasibility, studies |
| 💬 Reddit / Forums | Real user pain points, community skepticism |
| 🎥 YouTube | Product demos, expert breakdowns, reviews |

Adaptive depth — stops when confident, not when a timer runs out.

---

### Phase 3 — Critique + Verdict 💀

Delivers a full structured report:

#### 📊 Scorecard (8 Dimensions, each rated /10)
| Dimension | What's Being Judged |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Problem Clarity | Is the problem real, specific, and painful? |
| Market Size | Is there enough of a market to matter? |
| Solution Differentiation | Meaningfully better than alternatives? |
| Technical Feasibility | Can it actually be built, and at what cost? |
| Business Model Viability | Can it make money? Are unit economics sane? |
| Founder-Market Fit | Are YOU the right person to build this? |
| Timing | Right moment, or too early/late? |
| Adoption Likelihood | Will real people actually use/pay for this? |

#### 🔬 Deep Analysis
- ✅ What's actually strong (backed by research)
- ⚠️ Technical risks
- 💸 Economic risks
- 🚪 Adoption risks
- 🎯 Competitive risks
- 💭 Assumption Graveyard — the beliefs you haven't proven

#### 🏁 Verdict
One sentence. Build it, pivot it, or kill it — and exactly why.

#### 🛠️ 30-Day Fix List
Specific actions with measurable success signals. No generic advice.

| Weakness | Action | How to Know It Worked |
|----------|--------|-----------------------|
| Your flaw | Exactly what to do | What result proves it |

## ⚡ Adaptive Harshness

RealityCheck reads your confidence level during the interview and adjusts accordingly:

| Confidence Level | Critique Style |
|-----------------|---------------|
| 😌 Grounded & self-aware | Constructive, balanced, encouraging |
| 🤔 Somewhat overconfident | Direct, challenges assumptions clearly |
| 😤 Very overconfident | Full VC rejection mode — no mercy |
